We are Manufacture of Gold Machineries using world's Best Manufacturing practice.

Ravi Malviya Industries Pvt. Ltd. manufactures Gold Machinery using the outstanding Manufacturing Technique. RMI incepted in the year 1998 at Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Over the years with the hard work and foresight of Mr. Ravi Malviya the company has grown and diversified into areas of manufacturing, exporting, importing and trading a high-quality assortment of the Jewellery Machinery.

Today RMI is well accepted and trusted because we come up with novel and quality assured products to meet customerâ€™s requirements. In RMI modern, high-tech production facilities and close attention is paid to even the tiniest of details to ensure consistently high quality.

To meet the modern-time jewellery manufacturers expectations we offer a wide range of jewellery machines including Jewellery wire drawing machine,Wire & sheet rolling machine, Annealing machine, Jewellery bull block machine, Wire forming machine, Gold chain powder mixture, Gold draw bench machine, Pipe annealing machine , line center & hardening machine and other various tools & equipment.

We are the largest importer of jewellery machinery from Italy to offer technically advanced machinery for our Indian jewellery manufacturers. So, that they can take the benefit of every ounce of the latest technology at the best price.

We have a team of experts that provides complete training with regular guidance to take the preventive precaution of machine units for the zero breaks down and to enhance the life of machinery. Along with that, we do the entire setup of chain-making plant, hollow machine chain plant and other various manufacturing setups for the jewellery industry. We also offer technical support and take care of repairing & rebuilding of the old machinery as well.
